Advanced Installation Techniques What sets us apart is our commitment to using industry-leading methods to ensure longevity and stability in every project. One of our signature technologies is the Double Glue Down method. This innovative approach involves:

Laying a high-quality underlay as a foundation for soundproofing and cushioning.

Applying adhesive beneath the underlay to secure it to the subfloor.

Adding a second layer of glue on top of the underlay to bond the flooring material (like parquet) securely in place.

This technique is especially effective for parquet installations, including intricate designs like Chevron, Herringbone, and traditional plank layouts. The result? A floor that’s not only visually stunning but also built to withstand the test of time, even in Canada’s diverse climate.
